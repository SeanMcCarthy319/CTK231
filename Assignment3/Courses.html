Creative Technologies Courses (CTK)

200 Level Classes

201 – Basic Skills in the Digital Arts (3)

Introduction to the history, theories, current trends, practices, and core media in the digital arts.

202 – Foundations: Audio Theory (3)

Fundamentals of audio theory as applied to the physics and aesthetics of sound.

203 – Foundations: Recording and Sampling (3)

Aesthetics and technical skills for recording and sampling instruments and the human voice.

204 – Foundations: Music Concepts (3)

Foundational music concepts: basic theory, history, genres, and forms.

208 – Audio Production I (3)

Audio production techniques within a digital audio workstation (DAW) environment.

231 – Web Design and Development (3)

Theory, technical skills, and design concepts for standards-compliant websites.

232 – Computer Programming for Creatives (3)

Text-based, object-oriented programming for interactive design, UI, data viz, and games.

240 – Sound Design 1: Beginning Digital Audio (3)

Introduction to digital audio and aesthetic principles of sound.

260 – Arts Technology Performance Studio (3)

Creative, interdisciplinary performance integrating computer technology into music, dance, performance art, or theatre.

300 Level Classes

303 – Advanced Web Design and Development (3)

Advanced programming for dynamic websites and interactive web applications.

320 – Programming Dynamic Media (3)

Graphical, dataflow programming for performance, installation, and generative art/sound.

330 – Web Video (3)

Skills and concepts for fictional and documentary video content for the web.

340 – Sound Design 2: Advanced Digital Audio (3)

Advanced digital audio and aesthetic principles.

345 – Sequencing and Digital Audio (3)

Music synthesis via MIDI, digital sound editing/recording, and integrating digital audio and MIDI.

351 – Game Design I (3)

Foundational game design concepts and frameworks for engagement and fun.

354 – User Experience Design for Games (3)

Interface design trends in gaming and their influence on player experience.

377 – Motion Graphics (3)

Compositing, animation, and VFX for broadcast, web, VR/AR.
