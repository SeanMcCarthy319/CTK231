Retention Requirements

  • Audio & Music Production / Interdisciplinary Technologies — maintain an overall cumulative GPA of 2.50.
  • Game Design — maintain an overall 2.50 GPA and a 3.0 GPA within the major; maintain consecutive Fall–Spring enrollment in the final year or reapply.

Standards for Progress in the Major

Students may continue in the program if they maintain a cumulative GPA of 2.50 and demonstrate competency via an intermediate portfolio review. Additional expectations may be set by the program to ensure continuous progress and readiness for upper-division coursework.

Sequences in Creative Technologies

Accelerated Creative Technologies Sequence

High-achieving students (3.20+ GPA) may take up to 12 hours of approved graduate courses that count toward both degrees.

Audio and Music Production Sequence

Focuses on sound recording, audio production, sound design, music composition/theory, and entrepreneurial practices.

Game Design Sequence

Concentrates on game design and production with supporting electives across creative technology fields.

Interdisciplinary Technologies Sequence

Centers on mobile, web, programming concepts, UI/UX, motion graphics, and interactivity with broad elective options.
