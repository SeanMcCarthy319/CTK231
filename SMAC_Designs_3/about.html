About Sean

I am a designer who blends graphic design, UX and UI, and creative technologies to build systems that feel cohesive and human. My work covers branding, digital products, and multisensory research that asks how design can look, sound, and feel at the same time.

SMAC-Designs is my personal studio space where I explore client work, personal experiments, and long term ideas. I enjoy working on projects that sit between disciplines and that ask for both structure and play.

Fun fact: I can talk for a long time about typography and tacos in the same conversation.
Most productive zone: late night sketching sessions with a playlist on loop.
Creative recharge: local coffee shops, movie marathons, and long walks to reset my brain.
Design inspirations: film title sequences, album art, and strong visual identities that know what they are about.
